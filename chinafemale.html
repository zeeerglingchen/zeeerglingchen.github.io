Background



Logo design for Chinese Feminism, a research group was founded by scholars from the University of Michigan. They partner with institutions from China to record and share China's feminst movement. And they stand up for the human rights of women and girls.

The goal was to create an organic identity which can deliver the idea of nonviolent resistance. In the meantime, I want to express that Chinese Feminism is the pioneer and leader of feminsts in China.
